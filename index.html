What is Green Tea?

Green tea is made from unoxidized leaves and is one of the least processed types of tea. It contains antioxidants and other nutrients that have various health benefits.

Health Benefits

Rich in Antioxidants

Green tea is loaded with powerful antioxidants that help combat free radicals and prevent cell damage.

Boosts Metabolism

Studies have shown that green tea can help in increasing fat burning and improve metabolic rate.

Improves Brain Function

The caffeine and amino acid L-theanine in green tea can work together to improve brain function.

Brewing Tips

  • Use water that's just below boiling point (around 175°F or 80°C).
  • Brew for 2-3 minutes to avoid bitterness.
  • Use high-quality loose leaves for the best flavor.
